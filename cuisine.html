Cuisine

 

Gujarati cuisine is known for its diverse and flavorful dishes, many of which are vegetarian. Some of the famous Gujarati dishes include.

DHOKLA

Dhokla: A spongy and savory steamed cake made from fermented rice and chickpea flour, often served as a snack or breakfast item

 

 

 

  • Dabeli

It is a sweet snack made by mixing boiled potatoes with a special dabeli masala, putting the mixture in a ladi pav (burger bun), and serving it with chutneys made from tamarind, date, garlic, red chilies and other ingredients. It is garnished with pomegranate and roasted peanuts.

 

Thepla

Thepla is a soft Indian flatbread typical of Gujarati cuisine While extremely popular across Gujarat, it is especially common amongst the Jain community. It is served as breakfast, as a snack as well as a side dish with a meal. Its common ingredients are wheat flour, besan, methi and other spices
